# Sigmoidal Accuracy-to-Log-Likelihood Scaling Law Fit - Q&A

---

## Core Idea

**Q: Why can accuracy be sigmoidal versus loss?**
A: Accuracy is thresholded and saturates.

**Q: Why is log-likelihood smoother?**
A: It reflects probability assigned to the answer, not just correct/incorrect.

**Q: What does the midpoint represent?**
A: The loss region where accuracy changes fastest.
