# Power-Law Scaling Law - Q&A

---

## Core Idea

**Q: What is a power-law scaling law?**
A: A predictable relationship where loss improves like `A * scale^-alpha` plus a floor.

**Q: Why use log-log plots?**
A: A power law becomes a straight line after subtracting the floor.

**Q: What does alpha control?**
A: How quickly loss improves with scale.
