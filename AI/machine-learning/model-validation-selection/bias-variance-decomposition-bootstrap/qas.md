# Bias-Variance Decomposition from Bootstrap - Q&A

---

## Core Idea

**Q: What is bias?**
A: Systematic error of the average prediction.

**Q: What is variance?**
A: Sensitivity of predictions to the training sample.

**Q: Why use bootstrap?**
A: To simulate many alternative training sets from one dataset.

## Diagnosis

**Q: What suggests underfitting?**
A: High bias.

**Q: What suggests overfitting?**
A: High variance.
